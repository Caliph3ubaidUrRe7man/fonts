Universitype is a font foundry based in Makassar, Indonesia. It was established in 2024. The team consists of four passionate experienced type designers, they are Muh. Aswar, Ahmad Muzayyin Syarkawi, Andi Syahdang and Novan Arisandi, who specialize in crafting unique typography. They draw inspiration from Indonesia's rich cultural heritage and diverse landscapes to create distinctive and expressive fonts.
